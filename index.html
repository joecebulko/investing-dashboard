VIX — 30 Day Trend
VIX / VIX3M Term Structure — 60 Day Trend
Above 1.00 = backwardation (front-month panic, fat premiums but assignment risk). Below 1.00 = contango (normal).
Reading the VIX/VIX3M Term Structure Chart [close]
What it is
The ratio of front-month VIX (30-day vol) to VIX3M (90-day vol). Tells you the shape of the volatility curve — more informative than the level alone.
The four states
● <0.95 Deep Contango — boring, calm, skinny premiums. Standard regime. Trust your dashboard.
● 0.95-1.00 Mild Contango — normal range. ~80% of all trading days live here.
● 1.00-1.05 Backwardation — front-month stress > expected long-term vol. Premiums fatten meaningfully. Often the entry zone for experienced put sellers.
● >1.05 Deep Backwardation — genuine market stress. Premiums look spectacular. This is where retail blows up. Move to wider strikes (7-8% OTM) or stand down.
The six trajectories that matter
Flat in cyan — boring market. Standard system, premiums skinny.
Rising in contango (cyan→green slope up) — earliest warning of regime shift. Take only highest-conviction trades.
Crossing 1.00 from below (green→amber) — the flip. Premium-rich entry zone for disciplined sellers. Forward returns historically better than calm-market periods.
Climbing in backwardation (amber→red slope up) — stress intensifying. Discipline test. Wider strikes or stand down.
Falling from backwardation (red→amber→green slope down) — historically the highest-edge setup. Premiums elevated, fear retreating. Treasure these moments.
Bouncing around 1.00 — indecisive market. Stay conservative; don't read chop as "neutral".
Strike adjustments by state
Cyan / Green: standard 5% OTM
Amber falling: 5% OTM still fine — real edge
Amber rising: move to 6-7% OTM, smaller size
Red, regardless of direction: 7-8% OTM minimum or skip entirely
What it WON'T tell you
Direction. Backwardation can resolve up or down. Pair with regime + S&P trend.
Per-ticker risk. Market-wide signal. Don't override a NO-GO from your dashboard.
Timing. Curve can stay backwardated for weeks. Don't size up just because the indicator triggered.
Honest caveat
You haven't validated whether this indicator changes your decisions yet. Log every Monday's reading in your decision spreadsheet. After 6 months: if it never moved you off a trade, kill it.
